About us

  • SaddleBud was being developed a few years before its completion on September 2022. It was inspired after the owner attended various community colleges around LA and after seeing a need from students in community colleges.

    If you like the site, please don't forget to share it with your classmates. The more collaborative members join, the better the team gets, and that applies to life as much as it does in the classroom.

    SaddleBud is currently being introduced to local colleges so only certain students can join for now. But we're planning to slowly grow so contact us if you want to see your college here!
